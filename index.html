Welcome to RCHEM

During the year 1995 RChem was established as a proprietorship firm at SIDCO Industrial Area, Hosur, Tamil Nadu for manufacture of Sodium Azide. The said chemical compound has extensive applications as a drug intermediary, in safety air bags and is a vital ingredient for production of anti-Hypertensive and anti-AIDS drugs. The unit was allotted 20000 Sq.ft by SIDCO, Tamilnadu at Hosur and has infrastructure facilities for production of 99%+ pure Sodium Azide.

The unit has been receiving continuous and repetitive orders for supply of its product for the past two decades establishing its capability of producing and processing the product to the stringent specifications set by the consumers. The unit is assisted by qualified chemists and experienced technical staff in carrying out day-to-day operations.

Subsequently, to meet the demand of the product, another unit was established in the year 2012 at Somanahalli industrial area, an extent of 4 acres provided by KIADB, near Maddur of Karnataka. With this the combined capacity of manufacturing of the two units became 1000 TPA.

Products

Sodium Azide

Sodium Azide, NaN3 (SA) is an inorganic chemical, which exists as an odorless white solid. It is slightly basic, highly soluble in water and is slightly endothermic while dissolving. It is not friction-sensitive and also not an explosive. The chemical is quite stable in pH environs of 9 or more and is non-carcinogenic.

Sodium Azide is best known as the chemical found in automobile airbags. It is used as a chemical preservative in hospitals and laboratories. It is used in the manufacture of broad spectrum pre & post plant pesticides. It is also used in detonators and other explosives. It is used as an intermediate in the manufacture of pharmaceutical to treat hypertension, with no side effects. It is also used in the manufacture of Antivirals AZT, Tami flu, etc. Thus, Sodium Azide has a wide variety of applications in the chemical and pharmaceutical fields.
